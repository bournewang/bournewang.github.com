/ɪnˈdjuːs/
verb
1. To lead by persuasion or influence; incite or prevail upon.
2. To cause, bring about, lead to.
3. To cause or produce (electric current or a magnetic state) by a physical process of induction.
4. To infer by induction.
5. To lead in, bring in, introduce.
6. To draw on, place upon.
synonyms: entice, inveigle, put someone up to something, bring about, instigate, prompt, provoke, stimulate, trigger
