/ˈfɹeɪm.wɜːk/
noun
1. A support structure comprising joined parts or conglomerated particles and intervening open spaces of similar or larger size.
2. The arrangement of support beams that represent a building's general shape and size.
3. The larger branches of a tree that determine its shape.
4. A basic conceptual structure.
5. A reusable piece of code (and, sometimes, other utilities) providing a standard environment within which an application can be implemented.
6. The identification and categorisation of processes or steps that constitute a complex task or mindset in order to render explicit the tacit and implicit.
