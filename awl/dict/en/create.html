verb
1. To bring into existence; (sometimes in particular:)
2. To cause, to bring (a non-object) about by an action, behavior, or event, to occasion.
3. To confer or invest with a rank or title of nobility, to appoint, ordain or constitute.
4. To be or do something creative, imaginative, originative.
5. In theatre, to be the first performer of a role; to originate a character.
6. To make a fuss, complain; to shout.
adjective
Created, resulting from creation.
