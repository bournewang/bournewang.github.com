/dɪsˈkɹɪmɪneɪt/
verb
1. To make distinctions.
2. (construed with against) To make decisions based on prejudice.
3. To set apart as being different; to mark as different; to separate from another by discerning differences; to distinguish.
synonyms: disfavor, differentiate, distinguish
adjective
Having the difference marked; distinguished by certain tokens.
