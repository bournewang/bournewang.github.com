/ˈbɛn.ɪ.fɪt/
noun
1. An advantage; help or aid from something.
2. A payment made in accordance with an insurance policy or a public assistance scheme.
3. An event such as a performance, given to raise funds for some cause.
4. Beneficence; liberality
synonyms: advantage, aid, assistance, boon, foredeal, help, subsidy
verb
1. To be or to provide a benefit to.
2. To receive a benefit (from); to be a beneficiary.
synonyms: batten, behoove, help
