noun
1. (usually pluralized) Something which has exactly the same properties as something else.
2. An identical twin.
adjective
1. Bearing full likeness by having precisely the same set of characteristics; indistinguishable.
2. Not different or other; not another or others; not different as regards self; selfsame; numerically identical.
3. Of twins, sharing the same genetic code.
4. Exactly equivalent.
5. Approximating or approaching exact equivalence.
