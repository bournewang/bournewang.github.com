/əˈsaɪn/
noun
1. An assignee.
2. A thing relating or belonging to something else; an appurtenance.
3. An assignment or appointment.
4. A design or purpose.
verb
1. To designate or set apart something for some purpose.
to assign a day for trial
2. To appoint or select someone for some office.
to assign counsel for a prisoner
3. To allot or give something as a task.
4. To attribute or sort something into categories.
5. To transfer property, a legal right, etc., from one person to another.
6. To give (a value) to a variable.
We assign 100 to x.
synonyms: allocate, earmark, consign, convey
