[/ɹɪˈsɜːtʃ/][/ɹiˈsɝtʃ/]
noun
1. Diligent inquiry or examination to seek or revise facts, principles, theories, applications, etc.; laborious or continued search after truth.
2. A particular instance or piece of research.
synonyms: examination, exploration, inquiry, investigation, scrutiny, study
verb
1. To search or examine with continued care; to seek diligently.
2. To make an extensive investigation into.
3. To search again.
