noun
Something that has been differentiated or stratified.
verb
1. To show, or be the distinction between two things.
2. To perceive the difference between things; to discriminate.
3. To modify, or be modified.
4. To calculate the derivative of a function.
5. To calculate the differential of a function of multiple variables.
6. To produce distinct organs or to achieve specific functions by a process of development called differentiation.
