verb
1. To restrain within boundaries; to limit; to confine
After suffering diahrroea, the patient was restricted to a diet of rice, cold meat, and yoghurt.
2. (specifically) To consider (a function) as defined on a subset of its original domain.
synonyms: bound, circumscribe, coerce, curb, limit, repress, restrain, withstrain
adjective
Restricted.
